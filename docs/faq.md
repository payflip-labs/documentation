# FAQ

Common questions about using Payflip, grouped by topic.

---

## Usage

### Do I need multiple wallets?

No. Payflip gives you one balance to use from a single interface.

### Do I need to bridge funds?

No. Payflip is designed so you do not have to move funds before using them.

### Do I need to choose a chain or asset?

No. You interact with a unified balance without selecting chains, tokens, or gas settings.

### Do I need to understand crypto to use Payflip?

No. Payflip is designed so users do not need to understand chains, bridges, tokens, or gas.

### Can I still set fund preferences?

Yes. By default, Payflip requires no extra setup. If you want more control, you can set preferences without changing the simplicity of the experience.

---

## Recipients

### What do I enter to send a payment?

You enter an amount and a recipient, using a name or email address instead of a blockchain address.

### What does the recipient receive?

The recipient sees the payment in Payflip. The sender does not need to manage chain or token details.

### What happens if the recipient is not on Payflip?

You can still send them money using their email. When they join Payflip, the funds are available to them.

---

## Security & custody

### Is Payflip custodial?

No. Payflip is non-custodial. You retain control of your funds at all times.

---

## Fees & speed

### Are payments instant?

Payflip is designed for payments to feel immediate from the user's perspective.

### Are there fees?

Yes. Any fees are shown before you confirm, and you do not manage gas separately.
