# Future Directions

Payflip is designed as a foundational layer for how money is used across systems. Beyond simplifying payments, it can extend into additional capabilities.

---

## Fiat access (on-ramp / off-ramp)

Move seamlessly between traditional financial systems and on-chain balances.

- Add funds using local payment methods
- Withdraw funds to bank accounts
- No separate services or workflows to manage

---

## Privacy-preserving payments

Enable transactions where user activity and balances aren't publicly exposed, without sacrificing usability.

---

## Integrated yield

Earn yield on your balance without manually moving funds across protocols.

---

## Expanded chain and asset support

Support a broader set of ecosystems, assets, and execution environments as the landscape evolves.

---

## Advanced routing

Improve how value is sourced and routed across systems, optimizing for speed, cost, and reliability.

---

## Unified financial interface

Extend beyond payments into a complete interface for managing and using digital money across applications.
